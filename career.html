James's Career Page

My Experience

I currently am a Sales Associate at Bath & Body Works. This nine-billion-dollar company offers a variety of products ranging from body care items to home care. My job as a sales associate is to steer customers toward the products that suit their needs. We are encouraged to have a friendly tone and to keep a smile on our faces. I have been working for the company for over six months now. I decided to apply for this job because I have always been very good at persuading others to follow my advice, and I've always loved the products Bath & Body Works sells. From the cute gingham pattern that adorns the store to the friendly staff, I always enjoy clocking into my shifts.

Past Careers

Before working for Bath & Body Works, I held several other jobs. Most recently, I worked on campus at Elon University at the GEC. The GEC stands for the Global Education Center. I worked in the position of a front desk student assistant. Together the staff and I would help direct students to their advisors for whom they would consult about studying abroad. Our study abroad program at Elon University is one of the best in the United States. Before I came to Elon University, I worked at a deli catering company named "Apple Spice." This company produced boxed lunches for company meetings and parties. My job was to prepare the food and ensure the quality met the company's standards. One of the many things I have realized while working various jobs is my passion for communicating with others.
