Who am I ?

Hi, my name is James Silk. I am nineteen years old and am a sophomore currently attending Elon University. I am majoring in Journalism and minoring in Political Science. My passion for journalism is connecting with everyday people and discovering their stories. I have also been interested in politics because it significantly shapes my life as an LGBTQ+ American Citizen, which led me to my minor. I am interested in becoming a television news anchor. I've lived in Florida, Georgia, New Jersey, New York ... In addition, I hang out with my friends in my spare time, go shopping, and try new places to eat. I hope you enjoy learning more about my life through this webpage.
